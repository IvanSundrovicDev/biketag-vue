The BikeTag Project was started in 2018 as a tool for making the playing of the BikeTag Game easier on Reddit (r/CyclePDX) and was a simple form with two pictures and a couple of textboxes. The website was that form and an archive of all previously posted tag images. Since the launch of BikeTag 2.0 (the app you are using now) we now support the ability to set, save, and update GPS locations for BikeTag posts both newly created and of old.

However, the features to be able to update all of the GPS locations of old is not yet complete and thus we don't want to show you a broken map. For now, please enjoy this map of a boundary of the playing area for this game. A fully interactive map with pins of every single BikeTag Post will show up here soon!
